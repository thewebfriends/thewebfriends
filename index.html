Code of Conduct

A huge thanks to Marlena Compton and the PearConf Team as well as Chelsea Troy and the PromptConf Team, whose Codes of Conduct inspired ours. Our byline, harassment examples, and examples of what we won’t act on all come from their Codes of Conduct.

Web Friends prioritizes marginalized people’s safety over privileged people’s comfort. We do not tolerate harassment of anyone in the community, in or out of the Slack, online or offline.

You can find our entire Code of Conduct here.

About The Web Friends

The Web Friends is an online community for folks to engage and discuss all things important to the members participating within. We welcome all!

Our community is governed by a Code of Conduct.
